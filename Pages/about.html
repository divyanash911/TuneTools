What is this website?

TuneTools is a project taken up by two music fans as a part of their ISS course. This website features data about famous music artists , songs , albums and even top charts , and artists. It also helps to categorize music by artists or genres. The sole purpose of this project is to make an interactive page for music lovers.

What Tunetools can be used for?

TuneTools shows some of the OGs music creators in an organized way containing their albums,songs and many more. Tunetools can be expanded into showing ratings of various musics,albums and creators. Tunetools are one of a kind and is designed with a lot of hardwork by its creators.
